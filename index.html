Darkroom photography studio in Westchester County, NY

Kinochrome Studio provides film photography and darkroom services in Westchester County, NY. We are a very small and passionate team of professionals who fell in love with the darkroom.

We believe that the deliberate and artistic process of darkroom photography is our resistance to our digital world. We take fewer photos in order to focus on purpose and quality.

We believe in building our community and are committed to sharing the magic of darkroom photography with our friends and neighbors.
